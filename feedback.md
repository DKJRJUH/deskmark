Feedback & Support  
===  

`Deskmark` is a native macOS app designed to enhance your desktop with customizable text and icon watermarks, making it particularly useful while recording videos or during screen sharing. You can tile image and text watermarks across your desktop and customize their angle, text size, icon, spacing, and text color for a truly personalized watermark setup.

Your feedback and support are invaluable! If you encounter any issues, have questions, or would like to suggest new features, please feel free to reach out. Your input helps us continuously improve `Deskmark` and ensures we deliver the best possible user experience.  

You can contact us through the in-app feedback feature or by sending an email to **kennyiseeyou@gmail.com**. We are dedicated to providing timely support and ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience for you. Thank you for choosing Deskmark!
